Directions for Jumanji!

Welcome to Jumanji.
It is a game for those who seek to find
a way to leave their world behind.
You roll the dice to move your token.
the goal is to reach the end when Jumanji is spoken.
Take care during the game as many dangers await
in the dark heart of Africa to seal your fate.
After each dice role you will be presented with a situation.
You must choose a way to deal with it that will not negativey impact your station.
Should you survive you will be able to continue along on the game board.
If you die you will be sent back to the beginning which you cannot afford.
